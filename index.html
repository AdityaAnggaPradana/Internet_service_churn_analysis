Project Summary

This project aims to identify the customer churn rate and develop strategies to reduce this high churn rate. Utilizing the dataset titled "Internet Service Provider Customer Churn," our objective :

  • To investigate the factors influencing customer churn.
  • Establish correlations.
  • Predict customer churn using logistic regression modeling.
  • Provide actionable recommendations that can be utilized to shape our future strategies.

Project Background


O-Net Group is a leading internet service provider company. The CEO has assigned the Head of Data Analysis to conduct an in-depth analysis to identify the customer churn rate and formulate strategies to mitigate this high churn rate.

Data Overview and Cleaning

Data Cleaning

  1. We removed Irrelevant variables that we don't use in analysis .
  2. We removed duplicates data.
  3. We Handle Missing Value by impute value in it.
  4. We Identifying outliers by using interquartile methods and then removing the outliers.

Objectives

  1. To investigate the factors influencing customer churn.
  2. Establish correlations.
  3. Predict customer churn using logistic regression modeling.
  4. Provide actionable recommendations that can be utilized to shape our future strategies.

Insight

  1. The Internet-only package has the highest customer churn rate based on Customer Package Category.
  2. Customers in the subscription age of 0-1 year constitute the group with the highest number of customer churns..
  3. Surprisingly, Service Failure does not influence customer churn, as the majority of customer churns come from non-problematic service failures.

Recommendation

  1. Since the internet-only package has the highest churn rate, consider offering more customized packages. Understand the diverse needs and preferences of your customers and create packages that cater to their specific requirements. This could include options for different speeds, data limits, and pricing structures.
  2. Customers in the subscription age of 0-1 year have the highest churn rate. To address this, concentrate on improving the onboarding experience for new subscribers. Provide clear, concise information about the services, and ensure a smooth installation and setup process. Consider offering incentives or discounts for the first few months to encourage long-term commitment.
